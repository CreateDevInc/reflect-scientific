Mr. Boyce is the founder of Reflect Scientific and serves as President, Chief Executive Officer and Chairman of our Board of Directors. Mr. Boyce founded Reflect Scientific in 1993. Mr. Tait serves as Vice President. Mr. Tait brings experience with accelerated product development, "lean" process management tools, strategic market analysis and acquisition integration.
